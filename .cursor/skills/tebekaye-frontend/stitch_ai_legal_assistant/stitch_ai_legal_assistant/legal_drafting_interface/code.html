Master Services Agreement

This Master Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of October 24, 2023, by and between Lex Nova Systems, LLC ("Client") and Digital Dynamics Corp ("Contractor").

1. SERVICES

Contractor shall provide the services described in each Statement of Work executed by the parties from time to time under this Agreement.

2. COMPENSATION

Client shall pay Contractor the fees set forth in the applicable Statement of Work. All undisputed invoices are due within thirty (30) days of receipt.

3. TERMINATION

3.1 Termination for Convenience. Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time, for any reason or no reason, upon providing the other party with [X] days' prior written notice.

3.2 Termination for Cause. Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party breaches a material term of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within fifteen (15) days.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY

Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the other party's Confidential Information and not to disclose it to any third party except as required by law.
