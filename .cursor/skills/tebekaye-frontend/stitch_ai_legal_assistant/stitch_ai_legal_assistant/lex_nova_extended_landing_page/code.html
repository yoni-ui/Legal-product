FAQ

Is Lex Nova a replacement for a lawyer?

How do you handle data privacy?

We use bank-grade AES-256 encryption. Your documents are never used to train our base AI models, and you maintain 100% ownership of your data at all times.

Does it work internationally?

Yes, our systems are trained on legal frameworks across the US, EU, UK, and major Asian markets, supporting over 50 languages.
